This is a list of resources online that I have found to be helpful. These range from lecture notes to seminar information.

[Don Monk's homepage](http://math.colorado.edu/~monkd/). Under "Lecture Notes, etc." you can find highly valuable supplementary notes to Jech and Kunen, among others.

[New York Logic](https://nylogic.github.io/)

Miguel Moreno's [list](https://www.miguelmath.com/webminars.html) of online seminars and talks

European Set Theory Society's [Online Activities page](https://ests.wordpress.com/online-activities/)
